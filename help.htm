Purpose:

Provide a AI control of one or more units for the DnD Attack Wing Game

Setup:

Setup game per standard rules, choosing any units and or missions.

Usage:

For each AI controlled unit:
  1. Select the alignment of the the AI controlled unit
  2. Select the unit type for the AI controlled unit
  3. Pick a target (human controlled) unit for the AI unit to attack
  4. Select the location on the targeting ring that matches the direction of target (human) unit
  5. Move the AI Unit using the displayed maneuver
    • Choose the "Closing" maneuver if the Target unit is in range and facing towards the the AI unit
    • Choose the "Retreating" maneuver if the Target unit is in range and facing away from the AI unit
    • Choose the "Out-of-Range" maneuver if the Target unit is further than range 3 from the AI unit
    • Choose the "Stressed" maneuver if the AI unit has an exhaustion token.
  6. Choose Action
  7. Complete round per standard rules

Picking Target:

There are multiple options for picking targets
  1. Always pick the nearest possible target
  2. Randomize target by rolling a 6-sided die:
    • 1-2: take on the closest unit
    • 3-4: take on the most damaged unit
    • 5-6: take on the highest level enemy unit

Choosing action:

Choose first action that matches criteria, based on list on the main page for the selected unit
NOTE: Target is a free action for the AI, choose this plus first other action that is available in the list.

NOTES:

  • Always choose the human control unit(s) maneuvers, before choosing AI maneuvers
  • AI units never get stress token, but do not perform any actions after a RED maneuver.
  • For secondary weapons or optional pilot skills, always use them when applicable
